Introducing VISTARA VISTARA is a smart city located on the Deccan Plateau, known for its mostly flat land with a few small hills, rivers, and lakes nearby. The city has fertile red sandy and black cotton soil. Vistara enjoys a tropical savanna climate with hot summers, moderate monsoon rainfall, and mild winters, making it a warm and pleasant place to live.
