Logistics & Optimization Systems

1. Industrial Waste Collection Route Optimization System (R-COS)

This system uses geospatial data and machine learning to calculate the most efficient routes for industrial waste collection vehicles, minimizing travel time, fuel consumption, and operational costs.

Key DSA Applications:
  • Graph Algorithms: **Dijkstra's** or **A\*** for shortest path calculation between collection points.
  • Optimization: **Travelling Salesman Problem (TSP)** approximation (e.g., using **Nearest Neighbor Greedy** algorithm) for route sequencing.
  • Data Structure: **Adjacency List/Matrix** for representing the road network graph.
  • Alignment: SDG 9 (Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure) & SDG 12 (Responsible Consumption and Production).

2. Industrial Land Plot Allocation System (I-PAS)

A data-driven system to efficiently match incoming industries with optimal land parcels based on their requirements (area, proximity to utilities, environmental impact zones, and neighboring industries).

Key DSA Applications:
  • Search: **K-D Tree** or **R-Tree** for fast, multi-dimensional spatial searching of available plots and proximity queries.
  • Optimization: **Maximum Bipartite Matching** or **Assignment Problem** algorithms (e.g., **Hungarian Algorithm**) to match industries to plots based on weighted criteria.
  • Geometry: **Computational Geometry** algorithms (e.g., **Convex Hull**) for efficient plot boundary management.
  • Alignment: SDG 9 (Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure) & SDG 11 (Sustainable Cities and Communities).
